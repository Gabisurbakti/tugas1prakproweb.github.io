ABOUT Hi-Vi



HiVi! is an Indonesian music group formed in 2009. This music group consists of 4 members, namely Neida Aleida (vocals), Ilham (vocals), Febri (vocals/guitar) and Ezra (vocals/guitar). This band is in the pop genre. His first album was Say Hi! To HiVi! released in 2012. HIVI is fortunate to be able to constantly share our crafts over the years in this dynamic industry.


ALBUM Hi-Vi



Album Pertama SAY HI! TO HIVI! (2012 hingga 2014)
"The band, which formed in 2009, has hatched one debut album called Say Hi! To HiVi! which was released in 2012 by Universal Music Indonesia with the first single entitled "Indahnya Dirimu". The song created by Febrian Nindyo tells the story of a pair of teenagers who fall in love at first sight which makes them praise each other. The theme of love is indeed very pronounced on this album. In addition to "The Beauty of You", other songs such as "Steal", "Always at heart", "Eyes To The Heart", "Third Person" and one recycled song by the band The Groove called "Delusion". The singles from the first album that followed were "Third Person", "Steal", "Eyes to Heart". Their singles received a good response, especially from teenagers and their fans who were named "HiFriends". The singles "Third Person" and "Steal-Steal" got the opportunity to be the soundtrack in the film "Cinta Brontosaurus" starring Raditya Dika. In early 2014, they again released a single titled "Heartbeat". Their first single with mixed language lyrics (English - Indonesian). At the end of the year, they had the opportunity to tour 4 countries in Europe, namely the Netherlands, France, Germany and Switzerland to introduce Indonesian culture to the country."

Album Kedua KERETA KENCANA
After not releasing a new work for a long time, HIVI again released their work in the form of a single entitled "Siapkah Kau 'tuk Jatuh Cinta Lagi" released by Seven Music in April 2015, an opening single that signifies their return to the world of music which will also be followed by a second album in collaboration with Nino (RAN), Lale, and Ilman (Maliq & D'Essentials) as their producers. The song again received a good response from music connoisseurs from the country, through one of the videos with the highest number of viewers on Youtube (2015) and the number 2 song that was most often played on social media path (2015). At the end of 2015, HIVI continued its journey through the release of an upbeat single titled "Sama-Sama Tahu", which was also Dea's last single with HIVI before finally resigning in 2016, and has released sneak peek all the tracks on this album. In collaboration with Andi Rianto, Belanegara Abimanyu and the Laleilmanino trio Dea resigned in March 2016, and the HiVi! again complete with a new lead vocalist named Neida Aleida aka Neida who replaces Dea's position. With this new formation, HiVi! launched a new single titled, Cover Girl, As It Is, Teens, Rainbows, and Dating Trains from the album Dating Trains. The presence of Neida changed the image of HIVI, which was previously famous for its downtempo and sometimes somber songs, to be more cheerful, vibrant, and upbeat.
